SENV

Cybersécurité : Menaces et organisations, hygiène numérique

This course follows a peculiar format, based on Gildas Avoine' MOOC (link to the MOOC is given during the first class). Since half of the course is video-based, it is mostly organized in reverse-course: students are expected to watch the videos before the corresponding class. Classes will provide complementary information (updates and in-depth look at some notions), and be dedicated to answering the students questions regarding the videos.

Each class will end with a quiz on the videos.

Practical exercises and lab sessions will focus on complementary concepts.

Course Description

This course aims to raise students' awareness of cybersecurity, providing them with the knowledge, skills and best practices they need to avoid the main computer attacks, and thus evolve serenely in our digital world, whether in a professional or family context.

Topics covered include viruses, spam, passwords, information leakage, an introduction to cryptography, geolocation, digital certificates and more.

Course Organization:
