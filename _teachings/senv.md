## Course Description

This course follows a reverse-course format based on Gildas Avoine's cybersecurity MOOC. Students watch the videos before the corresponding class, and class time is used for complementary information, updates, deeper discussion, and questions.

Each class ends with a quiz on the videos. Practical exercises and lab sessions focus on complementary concepts.
