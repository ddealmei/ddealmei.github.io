This course follows a reverse-course format based on [Gildas Avoine](https://www.avoine.net/)'s cybersecurity MOOC. Students watch the videos before the corresponding class, and class time is used for complementary information, updates, deeper discussion, and questions.

Each class ends with a quiz on the videos. Practical exercises and lab sessions focus on complementary concepts.

## Course Description

This course aims to raise students' awareness of cybersecurity, providing them with the knowledge, skills and best practices they need to avoid the main computer attacks, and thus evolve serenely in our digital world, whether in a professional or family context.

Topics covered include viruses, spam, passwords, information leakage, an introduction to cryptography, geolocation, digital certificates and more.
