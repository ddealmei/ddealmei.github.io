This course follows a reverse-course format based on [Gildas Avoine](https://www.avoine.net/)'s cybersecurity MOOC. Students watch the videos before the corresponding class, and class time is used for complementary information, updates, deeper discussion, and questions.

Each class ends with a quiz on the videos. Practical exercises and lab sessions focus on complementary concepts.

## Course Description

This course gives students a practical cybersecurity culture for daily professional and personal use. It focuses on understanding common attacks, recognizing information leaks, and adopting habits that reduce exposure without requiring a specialist security background.

Topics include information leakage, spam, malware, passwords, certificates, cryptography, metadata, USB-based attacks, and practical privacy/security tradeoffs.
